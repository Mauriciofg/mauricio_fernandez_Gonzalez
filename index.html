I am a Biologist interested in the responses of organisms to environmental stressors. My research areas of interest are genomics, ecotoxicology, daphnia research, and cancer.

Scientific interests

Daphnia

I am interested in daphnia biology as ecotoxicological model organisms, to study changes in life-history traits as effect of environmental conditions.

Ecotoxicology

I have experience in ecotoxicology of heavy-metals and the effects of the exposure in life-history traits and gene transcription as response to environmental conditions.

Cancer

I am interested in cancer research as a phenomenon of uncontrolled energy allocation to growth.

Philosophy

I am primarily motivated by deep scientific thinking, which I distinguish from scientific opinion, mainly driven by abstracting time in silence nurtured by the ideas of real scientific thinkers such as Humberto Maturana and Francisco Varela.
